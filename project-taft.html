Project Overview

Taft Aesthetics required production of a complete construction document set for a two-story commercial interior in Wilson, NC, developed under the 2018 North Carolina State Building Code. Documentation spanned architectural, structural, life safety, and coordination drawings across an 8-sheet permit set.

Documentation Scope

Drawing production included life safety plan, foundation plan, roof plan, door and finish schedules, building sections, exterior elevations, and enlarged bathroom details. Sauna and ventilation coordination required resolving conflicts between mechanical requirements and the interior layout across revision cycles.

Process

Work was completed across multiple revision cycles — reviewing redline markups, updating Revit models, coordinating sheet references, and preparing PDFs for client and contractor review.

All construction drawings shown are the work product of Northline Architects. Displayed here with permission for portfolio purposes only. Hasini Kadaru contributed to this project as an Architectural Intern under licensed architect supervision.
