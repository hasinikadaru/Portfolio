Project Overview

Taft Aesthetics was one of the highest-hour projects completed during the Northline Architects internship. The scope required production of a complete construction document set for a commercial interior space, covering all major drawing types from life safety through detailed bathroom enlargements.

Documentation Scope

Drawing production included life safety plan, foundation plan, roof plan, door and finish schedules, building sections, exterior elevations, and enlarged bathroom details. Sauna and ventilation system coordination required close communication with the project lead to resolve conflicts between mechanical requirements and the interior layout.

Process

Work was completed across multiple revision cycles — reviewing redline markups, updating Revit models, coordinating sheet references, and preparing PDFs for client and contractor review.
