Project Overview

The Inner Loop Community Library and Wellness Space addressed the needs of an underserved urban community through a mixed-program civic building combining library services with wellness amenities.

The project was developed to construction documentation level within the MArch studio, producing a coordinated Revit set alongside physical study models that tested spatial and structural relationships.

Program & Design

The building program combined public library functions — reading rooms, stacks, children's area, community meeting rooms — with wellness spaces including fitness areas, counseling rooms, and quiet contemplation spaces.

Documentation

Floor plans, building sections, roof plan, and exterior elevations were produced at construction document level in Revit. Presentation drawings were developed from the model for studio review and critique.

Study Models

Physical study models were built at key design milestones to evaluate massing, structural logic, and the relationship between public and private program zones.
