Multi-unit residential apartment complex on a 40' × 40' corner site — efficient layout planning, furniture integration, and architectural visualization across typical, first, and third floor plans with cellar parking.

Project Overview

The Residential Flats at Alwal is a multi-unit apartment building on a corner site bounded by 40'-wide roads on two sides. The building accommodates two 3BHK units per floor across four floors above a cellar parking level, totaling eight residential units with shared vertical circulation.

Each 3BHK unit is organized around a central corridor with living, dining, and kitchen spaces toward the exterior and three bedrooms — including a master bedroom with attached dressing room and toilet — arranged for privacy and natural light access.

Unit Planning

The first floor plan shows two distinct unit types side by side, sharing a central corridor and lift core. Each unit includes a living room, dining area, kitchen with utility, pooja room, master bedroom with dressing and toilet, two additional bedrooms each with attached toilet, and a balcony. The typical floor plan (2nd–4th floors) consolidates this arrangement into a single efficient unit layout repeated on both sides of the core.

Cellar Parking

The cellar level provides structured parking for 10 vehicles across two bays, accessed via two gates from the 40' road frontage. A lift and staircase connect the cellar to all upper floors.
