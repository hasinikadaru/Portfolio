Project Overview

Community Bank N.A. required full tenant fit-out documentation for a third-floor commercial banking office at 3300 Monroe Avenue, Rochester, NY. The project was developed under the New York State Building Code 2020 and involved both existing condition documentation through an on-site field measurement and complete construction drawings for the proposed new layout.

The proposed program included open workstation areas, private offices, training rooms, a large conference room, break room with millwork, and a reception area — documented across a coordinated 8-sheet construction set. The scale of the floor plate and number of rooms required careful sheet organization and drawing reference management throughout the project.

Documentation Scope

Drawing production included demolition plan documenting all existing partitions, ceilings, and systems to be removed; proposed floor plan organizing open workstations, private offices, training rooms, conference rooms, break room, and reception across the third floor; life safety plan with egress path verification and exit width confirmation; reflected ceiling plan with lighting fixture schedule; finish plan with interior millwork elevations for copy/print stations and break room cabinetry; door schedule with hardware sets and frame types; interior window elevations; and accessibility standards sheet. All drawings were produced at 1/8" = 1'-0" scale across 8 coordinated sheets.

Revision Process

Work began with an on-site field measurement on September 17, 2025 — physically measuring existing conditions across the third floor and recording dimensions over base plans to verify measurement accuracy prior to Revit modeling. Construction documents were then developed in Revit across multiple revision cycles, incorporating architect feedback on room layouts, ceiling heights, lighting coordination, and millwork details to prepare a complete permit-ready set.
