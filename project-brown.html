Project Overview

Brown Residence traced the full design-to-documentation arc for a residential addition — beginning with hand sketches provided by the project lead and concluding with a complete Revit model and coordinated floor plan set.

The project provided experience with translating informal design intent into a building information model, managing spatial relationships in Revit, and producing a coordinated set of residential drawings.

Revit Workflow

The process began with sketch interpretation, establishing the structural grid and room layout in Revit. Floor plans were developed with dimensions, door and window tags, and room labels. Furniture was added to proposed plans to demonstrate spatial livability.

3D Model

A complete Revit 3D model was developed alongside the 2D documentation, allowing section views and perspective studies to be generated directly from the model.
